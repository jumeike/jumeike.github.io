About me

I am a 2nd Year computer science PhD student at the University of Kansas advised by Prof. Mohammad Alian. In my work so far in the Architecture Research Group, we have explored the optimization of software-based architectural simulators to enable efficient computer design and reduce energy costs. I am also working on “Device-less Networking” which investigates efficient CPU architecture for improved I/O performance.

Research Interest

  • Computer Architecture
  • Data Center Networking
  • Operating Systems
  • Emerging Technologies
